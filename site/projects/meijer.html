Meijer

http://www.meijer.com

The redesign of the e-commerce site, Meijer.com, was a huge task and a collaboration between myself, a visual designer, and Meijer's in-house development team.

The Meijer team defined a new taxonomy and I ran user testing via Treejack/Optimal Sort for validation. I started with a UX Strategy that included persona definition, a design strategy for focusing on lifestyle over products, and use cases to guide my wireframing.

I created wireframes for every section of Meijer.com from the homepage all the way through the purchase process.

Along with the .com redesign, I worked on many campaigns for Meijer, including this Pinterest contest for Back to School.
