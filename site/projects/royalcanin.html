Royal Canin

Royal Canin requested a new marketing site in order to launch a re-branded dog DNA test. We also developed new packaging, a brochure, and DNA report.

My first goal was to understand the audience for this product. Since Royal Canin is a pet food brand, I was curious to see if our personas were still correct for a dog DNA product. Using a mountain of quantitative research from Royal Canin, becoming a customer myself, and performing interviews of potential and past customers of the DNA product, I put together a customer journey map and crafted a primary persona.

After a work session with the Consumer and Veterinary clients, we developed a list of questions that our brochure and marketing site would need to answer. From this, I developed a content strategy document that outlined the content for each communication channel. From there, I provided a content hierarchy with design direction for the marketing site as well as some ideas for how this product could grow in the future.
