Spent

http://www.playspent.org

For Spent, I worked with the programmer to create the game design and logic based on the concept of a Sims-like choose-your-own-adventure game to help build empathy for those living in poverty. We created the game interface, structure, and guided content. I built a prototype in Mockingbird to test the game interface and functionality and led user research to test content once the game engine was created.

I also collaborated on the PR and social plan to get the word out about Spent. We had no media budget, but Spent has been played over 4 million times in over 200 countries to date.

Spent has won awards in multiple categories including: digital, PR, media, games, innovation, and non-profits.

New branding and a new fundraising effort for Urban Ministries of Durham will launch soon: namesforchange.org.
