Dognition

Dognition is a NC start-up that offers a revolutionary new tool that reveals your dog's unique style of thinking. By using cognitive science, Dognition can reveal how an individual dog interacts with his people and surroundings. I worked with scientists and researchers from Duke University to balance great customer experience with the scientific method, based on design principles and information design. I worked on a vast spectrum of tasks from concepting to marketing to instructional and information design, video storyboarding, complex logic systems, and user portal experiences along with content strategy and creation of content.

SPENT for Urban Ministries of Durham

Even in our challenging economic times, many don’t think they could ever end up homeless. We created Spent to let everyone walk for a virtual month in the shoes of someone living on the edge of homelessness. With over 2.5 million plays, we raised awareness and built empathy for those that Urban Ministries serves. I partnered with developers to create the game design, interaction and interface design, marketing strategy, user research and testing, and content creation.
